LovingKindness is all we need, to stay calm indeed.

“Compassion isn’t about solutions. It’s about giving all the love that you’ve got.” —Cheryl Strayed.

“LovingKindness” is a term given to us by Buddha and his disciples such as Thich Nhat Hanh. LovingKindness or true love starts with “Maitri” or friendship with oneself, with those closest to us and those who are not as well as our enemies. And Friendship is about being forgiving and kind to all of these people, starting with ourselves. From Maitri we travel to the emotions of Karuna (compassion), Mudita (joy) and Equanimity (freedom).

Celebrating Lovingkindness

We celebrate lovingkindness instead of festivals because it embodies all the virtues the festivals stand for. The excitement to celebrate this event begins few months ago and it energizes us and keeps us motivated ..
